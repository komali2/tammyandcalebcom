A brief and entirely objective account of how we got here.

2017

The Beginning

Caleb and Tammy first met on Tinder in 2017 — Tammy intrigued by Caleb's bold profile claim that he could speak Mandarin. After a chaotic start involving a Muse concert, many outrageous food purchases, and convincing Tammy to get her own motorcycle, their relationship blossomed and solidified.

2019

First Trip to Taiwan

Although Caleb had lived in Taiwan previously around 2014, they first visited Taiwan together in April 2019. It was Caleb's secret mission to convince Tammy to move there with him someday. One of their most memorable moments from that trip: renting two motorcycles, riding up Yangmingshan where Tammy dropped hers on a hill, and still somehow ending up at Yehliu Geopark — where Caleb showed Tammy one of his favorite hidden coastal hikes.

2021

Making Taiwan Home

After the COVID-19 pandemic and the end of Trump's first term, Tammy decided she had had enough of the United States and agreed to move to Taiwan with Caleb. They landed on January 6, 2021 — the same day as the U.S. Capitol insurrection — a symbolic event that only cemented their confidence that they had made the right choice.

2021–2023

Life in Taipei

In the years since, Caleb and Tammy have crossed 50 national borders together, opened a restaurant, loved and lost plants, fish, and lizards (RIP Richard), and shared hundreds of wonderful meals in beautiful places. They've grown deeply as a couple while supporting each other's individual journeys — through career changes, the closure of the restaurant, Caleb's parents first moving to and then moving away from Taiwan, and countless other challenges and triumphs — all motivated by their shared pursuit of personal growth, a thriving relationship, and the world's best chocolate cake.

2023

The Proposal

On April 27, 2023, Caleb and Tammy returned to the Yehliu Geopark hike they had done during their very first trip to Taiwan. At the end of the trail, at the terminal gazebo overlooking the ocean and cliffside — the very same one Tammy once called "so romantic" four years earlier — Caleb proposed. She said yes.

2026

Why Taiwan

Their wedding was originally planned for 2024 in the United States. After a wild business endeavor that involved opening a restaurant in Taipei and their continued disillusionment with U.S. politics, they decided to host their wedding in Taiwan instead — to celebrate in and honor the place they truly call home.
