Venue

HARUHANA Botanical Garden & Café

Tamsui, New Taipei City, Taiwan
Google Maps

Nestled among lush greenery on the hills of Tamsui, HARUHANA is a botanical garden and café venue offering a serene, garden-surrounded setting — the perfect backdrop for a celebration.

Dress Code

Our wedding dress code is cocktail attire. For women, this can be a nice dress that's floor, midi, or knee-length, a dressy jumpsuit, paired with heels, dressy sandals, or flats. For men, a button-up shirt and tie/bowtie, with or without a blazer, finished with trousers or chinos, with dress shoes or a clean pair of sneakers. If neither or both, just pick some combination of the aformentioned clothes that suits you, please!

Things to Note

No need to bring gifts or red envelopes. This is a destination wedding, and your presence with us on our special day is a present enough!

Wedding speeches during dinner will be pre-planned. We kindly ask to refrain from impromptu, surprise speeches. Save them for our karaoke night :)
