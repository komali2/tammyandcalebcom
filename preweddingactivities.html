We're planning a series of friend events during the week leading up to and after the wedding — a chance to explore Taiwan together and spend time in a more relaxed setting. Caleb and Tammy will likely be at most, but maybe not all, of the events, as our friends and family have graciously volunteered to help us host some of these activities.

Here is our tentative schedule of social activities, to be finalized closer to the event. We will be creating WhatsApp groups per event to coordinate. These activities are entirely optional!"

Wulai Day Trip

Caleb's favorite place in Taiwan, an indigenous hot spring town in the mountains just south of Taipei. Enjoy bathing in the natural riverside hot spring and seeing a gorgeous waterfall.

Wednesday, October 14

Riverside Bike Ride

Come join us on YouBikes for a scenic ride along Taipei's riverside. We will start around Gongguan and end in Bitan, Xindian. Afterwards, we will have a casual, classic Taiwan stir-fry dinner (熱潮).

Thursday, October 15

Bouquet-making and Picnic

An casual gathering at Da'an Park, Taipei's best picnic spot. Tammy will buy a bunch of assorted flowers beforehand so that friends/family can contribute to the wedding bouquet, as well as make your own boutonnieres or corsages.

Friday, October 16

Public Hot Spring

We will be relaxing at one of Beitou's public hot springs (likely Spring City Resort) in the morning and afternoon after the wedding. Bring your swim caps (or be prepared to rent on-site) and swimwear if you'd like to join us! Afterwards, we plan on going to eat at a hot pot restaurant.

Sunday, October 18

KTV Night

Karaoke at one of our favorite fancy venues in Taipei, "Oncor." Tammy will book the room in advance so please let her know if you're 80% sure you'd like to attend!

Monday, October 19
